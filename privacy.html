Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Fire Extinguisher Health Checkup Audit Tool ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your information.

2. Information We Collect

Our tool operates entirely within your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. The following information is processed locally:

  • Audit Data: Information you enter into the audit forms (company details, extinguisher information, hazard assessments)
  • Local Storage: Data is stored in your browser's localStorage for convenience
  • Usage Data: Anonymous usage statistics through Google Analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the audit tool functionality
  • To generate audit reports and PDF exports
  • To improve the tool based on usage patterns
  • To ensure proper functioning of the application

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. This means:

  • Data remains on your device
  • Data is not transmitted to our servers
  • You can clear this data at any time through browser settings
  • Data is specific to your browser and device

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For anonymous usage statistics
  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements
  • jsPDF: For PDF generation (client-side only)
  • Font Awesome: For icons
  • Google Fonts: For typography

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all data stored in your browser
  • Deletion: Clear all data using the "Clear All Items" button or browser settings
  • Export: Export your data as CSV or PDF
  • Control: Choose what data to enter and store

7. Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Client-side data processing only
  • No server-side data storage
  • Secure browser storage mechanisms
  • Regular security updates

8. Children's Privacy

Our tool is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
